Qualtrics

In the Department of Psychology we use Qualtrics to create questionnaires for research. If you are a student in the department you can self-enrol for an account with Qualtrics. It is important that you do this in the correct way so follow the instructions carefully.

  • Use this link https://liverpoolhope.eu.qualtrics.com/ to go to Qualtrics website
  • Click the link that says "Don't have an account?"
  • Enter your @hope.ac.uk email address
  • Enter the Access Code from the Departmental Moodle

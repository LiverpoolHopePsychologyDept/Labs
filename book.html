SONA Lab Bookings

Booking a Lab for Dissertation, Masters, & PhD Research

After you have obtained Lab Access, you can use SONA, a system for booking lab space and advertise your study to student participants from the Level C and M student participant pool. As you progress to Level H, your account is converted to a researcher account, enabling you to create your dissertation study, book lab space, and recruit participants for your research.

SONA Website: Log in using your @hope credentials.

How to use SONA

Below is a quick start user guide to help you set up your study in the correct way, add timeslots and recruit participants to your study. You are advised to read the instructions carefully. If your study is not set up correctly it will not be advertised to potential participants.

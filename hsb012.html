Transcription Suite

Back to main lab page

This suite has 4 PCs desicated to supporting qualitative data analysis. NVivo is installed on three of the PCs.

In terms of equipment for use by staff and students there are a number of data recording and transcribing tools available, such as cameras, telephone recording equipment, digital voice recorders, tripods and Olympus transcription kits.
